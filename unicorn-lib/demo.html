💡 Utility Class Reference

Complete list of available utility classes:

Spacing:
  • m-*, mt-*, mr-*, mb-*, ml-*, mx-*, my-*
  • p-*, pt-*, pr-*, pb-*, pl-*, px-*, py-*
  • Sizes: 0, xs, sm, md, lg, xl, 2xl, 3xl, 1-24
Flexbox:
  • flex, inline-flex
  • flex-row, flex-col, flex-wrap
  • justify-*, items-*, self-*
  • gap-* (same sizes as spacing)
Grid:
  • grid-cols-* (1-12), grid-rows-* (1-6)
  • cols-*, rows-*
  • col-start-*, col-end-*, row-start-*, row-end-*
  • gap-*, gap-x-*, gap-y-* (spacing scale)
  • place-items-*, place-content-*, place-self-*
Sizing:
  • w-*, h-* (auto, full, screen)
  • w-1/2, w-1/3, w-2/3, w-1/4, w-3/4
  • min-w-*, max-w-*, min-h-*, max-h-*
Position:
  • relative, absolute, fixed, sticky
  • top-0, right-0, bottom-0, left-0
  • inset-0, z-* (0, 10, 20, 30, 40, 50)

All components automatically adapt to light and dark themes. The theme follows your system preference or can be controlled manually using the data-theme attribute.
